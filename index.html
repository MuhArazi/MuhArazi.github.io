About Me

My name is Faiz, I study at SMK MUHAMMADIYAH KRAMAT, I like to draw. A cup of coffee brings me peace, and gives me an idea, thinking about what I will do to start the day to success.

I realized the potential within myself to be studied and explored more deeply. And I have found a thing that is in my potential, since childhood I have loved art, both music and other arts. But I'm not talking about that, I'm more interested in painting, I think Painting has a meaning or message that is in the object being drawn . Beautiful paintings can make the heart feel comfortable, paintings can symbolize sadness, happiness, sadness, warmth and other feelings, depending on what things we paint, and what we mean.

Once upon a time I found a sense of inferiority within myself that my work was nothing compared to the work of others out there, but I tried to think to convince myself that I am just starting this, I will be more than this for days to come, I believe that no no one will be successful if he does not find a challenge in his life to conquer, so that in the end he will find the success he will achieve for what he has worked for.

When you trust to yourself that you can do it, I believe that you can to be succes.
